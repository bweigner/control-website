Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 10, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Control: Bluffing & Territory ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or any personal details to use.

Local Data Storage

The App stores game data (such as career stats, achievements, settings, and preferences) locally on your device using your browser's or app's local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data or using the in-app "Reset All Progress" option

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information and ad interaction data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views)

Where required by law (such as in the EU/UK under GDPR), we will request your consent before serving personalized advertisements. You may opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

Analytics

The App does not currently use any analytics services. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal information from any user, no special provisions are required.

Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to external websites (such as this privacy policy page). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
